Heavy rain anchors a heavy scene in “The Highwaymen,” a look at the Bonnie and Clyde story from the perspective of the lawmakers hunting them down. Kevin Costner plays Frank Hamer and Woody Harrelson plays Maney Gault, two former Texas Rangers brought out of retirement to chase the notorious criminals. Here, the two come across the body of a murdered convict they had paroled to help them in their search.
