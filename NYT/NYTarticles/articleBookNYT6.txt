To the Editor:Re “ ‘Green Book’ Takes Home Best Film Award”(Arts pages, Feb. 25):The controversy regarding this year’s best picture award may not be a bad thing, as it gives more attention to “Green Book,” resulting in more people seeing the film.In these times, people are longing to see films about racial reconciliation. Even if unrealistically overdone in “Green Book,” the film models positive changes of thinking and behavior.Also, it provides an opportunity to learn about our nation’s history. I had never heard about the Green Book before seeing this movie. Jeffrey B. FreedmanNew YorkAdvertisement
