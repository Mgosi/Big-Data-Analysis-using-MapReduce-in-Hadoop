Ke$ha claims her fourth No. 1 on the ranking (also among nine top 10s). Her debut single "TiK ToK" ruled for seven weeks in 2010, followed by "Your Love Is My Drug" (two weeks, 2010) and "Die Young" (three weeks, 2012).
