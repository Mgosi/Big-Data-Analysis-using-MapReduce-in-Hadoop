Long Beach based trio half•alive made their show-stopping TV debut on Thursday night’s (March 14) episode of Jimmy Kimmel Live!, and announced a world tour one day later.The trio gave a spectacularly orchestrated performance of their danceable, funk-pop tune "Still Feel" that echoed their '70s-inspired music video for the track.Kicking things off with Jordan Johnson and Aidan Carberry of JA collective dancing on a staircase, lead vocalist Josh Taylor then enterered to revive trumpeters and tromboners as he twirled from the hall to other sets with waiting musicians.
