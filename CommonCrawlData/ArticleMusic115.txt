Lorde continued her reign of pop stardom with a live performance of "Tennis Court" at the 2014 Billboard Music Awards on Sunday (May 18).Following a spirited introduction from Nicki Minaj, Lorde took the stage dressed in a striking all-black ensemble, complete with a wide-brimmed Zorro-style hat. Close-ups showed the singer squeezing the emotion out of every word, as she performed the electro-goth song.The performance marked the first ever televised rendition of the song, which appears on Lorde's 2013 debut album "Pure Heroine."The 17-year old New Zealand native has been riding high since "Royals" became a surprise hit last year. It eventually topped the Hot 100 and earned the Grammy for Song of the Year. Subsequent singles "Team" and "Tennis Court" have peaked at 6 ant 71, respectively.Billboard Music Awards 2014: Red Carpet PhotosLorde is tied with Imagine Dragons for being a finalist in the most categories at this year's Billboard Music Awards, and in the first half of the show she won Top New Artist and Top Rock Song, for "Royals."The online extension of Billboard Magazine, billboard.biz is the essential online destination for the music business.Learn More© 2019 Billboard.
