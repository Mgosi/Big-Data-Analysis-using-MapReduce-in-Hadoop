Marco Antonio Solis spends his first month at No. 1 on Billboard’s Top Facebook Live Videos chart, ranking at Nos. 1, 3 and 9 on the February 2019-dated ranking.The chart, the latest of which recaps February 2019 activity, is a monthly look at the widest-reaching and most-reacted-to videos posted by musicians on Facebook Live, as tracked by media analytics company Shareablee. Rankings are determined by a formula that blends reactions, comments, shares and first-seven-days views.Solis, who has 11 No. 1s to his name on Billboard’s Hot Latin Songs chart between 1995 and 2014, rules the latest tally with a clip of his Feb. 27 set at the Vina del Mar Festival in Chile. It was the most-viewed video in its first seven days of any clip on the February 2019 ranking, earning 865,000 views, according to Shareablee, alongside 34,000 reactions, 9,000 comments and 9,000 shares.
