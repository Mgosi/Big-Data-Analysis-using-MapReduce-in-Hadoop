Ever since coming out as bisexual in 2012, R&B superstar Frank Ocean has made it a habit to not speak out very frequently about his sexuality, or about anything involving his personal life. But over the weekend, the star made a clear statement on his Instagram.On Sunday, Ocean shared an image on his Instagram Story (which has since disappeared) of a brand-new tattoo on his thigh, covering up an older, faded tattoo. The new image is a replica of an old illustration by The Simpsons creator Matt Groening.
