The second edition of the Bellwether Music Festival features a roster packed with beloved indie rock bands, including headliners Beach House, Cake, Cold War Kids and Guided By Voices. The event that takes place in the picturesque Renaissance Park in Waynesville, Ohio -- adjacent to the Renaissance Festival grounds -- will feature more than 20 bands taking two stages on Aug. 9-10, as well as the addition of "full contact rock n' roll jousting" and after parties. Other acts playing the camping festival include: Twin Shadow, STRFKR, Real Estate, Pinback, Black Moth Super Rainbow, Caroline Rose, Sego, Arlie, The Brook & The Bluff, Katie Pruitt, Hailey Knox and more. Early-bird passes, including 2-day GA tickets and 2-day VIP passes are on sale now here.Last year's inaugural event from the founder of Cincinnati's Bunbury Festival featured sets from The Flaming Lips, Local Natives, Dr. Dog, Echo & the Bunnymen, The Psychedelic Furs and Japanese Breakfast, among others.Check out the full 2019 lineup below.Your 2019 Bellwether Lineup -- Join us at the Ohio Renaissance Festival Grounds August 9th & 10th for camping and performances from @beaccchhoussse, CAKE, @coldwarkids, Guided By Voices, @strfkr_music and more! 2018 discount codes are valid until 11:00pm tonight.A post shared by  Bellwether (@bellwetherfest) on Mar 21, 2019 at 8:00am PDTThe online extension of Billboard Magazine, billboard.biz is the essential online destination for the music business.Learn More© 2019 Billboard.
