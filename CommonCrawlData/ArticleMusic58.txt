Hot off of his Not Dead Yet! tour, Phil Collins will return to the States for an extended run of shows, which he has cleverly dubbed the Still Not Dead Yet, Live! tour.The run will kick off in September in Dallas at the American Airlines Center and wrap up in October at Las Vegas's T-Mobile Arena. Tickets go on sale March 30, and fans can register for a March 24 pre-sale here.
