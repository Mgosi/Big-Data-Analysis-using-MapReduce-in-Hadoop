On Thursday night (March 21), leading urban artists gathered at the Coliseo de Puerto Rico to celebrate the first annual Tu Música Urban Awards. Ozuna led all artists with six awards, followed by Daddy Yankee and Natti Natasha with three awards apiece.   Tu Música celebrated urban music from the place where the genre was born, Puerto Rico. The event, produced by Soraya Sánchez and Mr. & Mrs. Entertainment, aired live on Telemundo Puerto Rico.
