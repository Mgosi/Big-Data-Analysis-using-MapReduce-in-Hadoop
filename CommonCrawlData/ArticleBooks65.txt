About the Book

The View From Your Window is a daily digital photo feature on the Daily Dish, one of the oldest and most popular blogs on the web, edited by The Atlantic's Andrew Sullivan, and published by the Atlantic.com. Among the blog's million monthly readers, many send in pictures of the window they look out of each day - as a way to capture the reality of actual life in a virtual world. Started three years ago, it's finally in book-form. Inside, Sullivan and his colleagues, Patrick Appel and Chris Bodenner, have selected 190 of their favorites, representing more than 80 countries and all 50 U.S. states, ordered from dawn to dusk.
